Each class can have its **own community space** — a discussion group where the cohort talks, asks questions and supports one another.

## Linking groups to the class

In the [class settings](/help-center/en/turmas/creating-a-class), use the **Community groups** field to select one or more groups.

When they enroll, students get **automatic access** to the linked groups. When they lose access to the class, their group access goes with it.

<Note>
Only groups with the access mode **linked to classes/courses** can be tied to a class. Create and manage groups in the community area.
</Note>

## What students can do

- Create **posts** in the group feed
- **Comment** and reply
- **Mention** other students with `@`

Mentions trigger an email and in-app notification for whoever is mentioned.

## External channel (WhatsApp, Telegram...)

In addition to the internal community, you can provide an external **communication channel** in the class settings (a WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord or Slack link), shown to students.
