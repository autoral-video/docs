The **engagement dashboard** shows how each student in the class is progressing and highlights who is **at risk** of dropping out — the heart of active support.

Access it in **Admin → Classes → [Class] → Dashboard**.

```
/admin/classes/{class}/dashboard
```

## Health indicator

Each student gets a health status:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟢 **Green** | Healthy engagement. |
| 🟡 **Yellow** | Needs attention. |
| 🔴 **Red** | Critical — little or no engagement. |

The summary cards show the total number of active students and the count in each status.

## Per-student metrics

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Lessons %** | Percentage of lessons completed. |
| **Modules %** | Percentage of modules completed. |
| **Assignments %** | Percentage of assignments approved. |
| **Posts 7d** | Community posts in the last 7 days. |
| **Last activity** | Date of the last activity in lessons. |

## Risk signals

The system flags, for example: days without activity, incomplete modules, open assignments without a submission, and zero community participation — so you can step in before a student drops out.

## Filter, export and recalculate

- **Search** by name/email and **filter** by status (green/yellow/red)
- **Sort** by last activity
- **Export CSV** with the engagement data
- **Recalculate** to refresh the numbers (shows the date of the last calculation)
