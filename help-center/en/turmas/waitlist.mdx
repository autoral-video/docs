---
title: "Waitlist"
description: "Capture interested people when a class runs out of seats and promote them to enrollment with an automatic link and acceptance deadline."
---

When a class with limited seats fills up, instead of simply turning away interested people, you can capture them on a **waitlist** and promote them when a seat opens.

## Enabling the waitlist

In the [class settings](/help-center/en/turmas/creating-a-class):

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Enable waitlist** | Turns on the waiting queue for the class. |
| **Maximum capacity** | Limit of people in the queue. Empty = no limit. |
| **Acceptance window (hours)** | Time the promoted person has to confirm (1 to 168 hours). |
| **Promotion checkout** | Which sales link to use when promoting. If empty, uses the checkout the customer originally tried. |

## How it works

1. The class is full. The interested person joins the queue and receives the **waitlist entry** notification.
2. When a seat opens, you **promote** someone from the queue in **Admin → Classes → [Class] → Waitlist**.

```
/admin/classes/{class}/waitlist
```

3. The promoted person receives an email with a **time-limited confirmation link** (the acceptance window).
4. If they confirm in time, they enroll. If the deadline expires, the seat becomes available again.

## Queue status

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Waiting** | In the queue. |
| **Promoted** | Received the confirmation link; within the deadline. |
| **Enrolled** | Confirmed and joined the class. |
| **Declined** | Declined or missed the deadline. |

The queue keeps each person's **position** and form data (e.g. phone number).

## Notifications

| Notification | When |
|---|---|
| Waitlist entry | when joining the queue |
| Waitlist promotion | when promoted, with the acceptance deadline |
