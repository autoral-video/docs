title: "Mentors and tutors"
description: "Build the class team by assigning mentors, co-mentors and tutors, with access restricted to each one's own classes."
---

A class can have a team that supports students: **mentors, co-mentors and tutors**.

Manage it in **Admin → Classes → [Class] → Mentors**.

```
/admin/classes/{class}/mentors
```

## Roles

| Role | Function |
|---|---|
| **Mentor** | Lead instructor of the class. |
| **Co-mentor** | Co-instructor. |
| **Tutor** | Additional support. |

## Adding a team member

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Mentor's email** | Email of an already registered user. |
| **Role** | Mentor, co-mentor or tutor. |
| **Notes** | Notes about responsibilities (optional). |

Click to add. The join date is recorded automatically.

## Removing a member

Removing a mentor records the **leave date** — they stop appearing as active, but the history is preserved.

## Mentor access to the admin panel

Mentors with the right permission can access the admin panel, but they **only see the classes they are assigned to** — including the students, calendar, assignments and reviews of that class. This is how you delegate the day-to-day support without granting full access to the platform.
