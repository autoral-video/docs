---
title: "My certificate (student)"
description: "Track your progress toward the class certificate, check eligibility and download the certificate once earned."
---

If your class issues a certificate, you track your progress under **Certificate**, inside the class.

```
/member/class/{class}/certificate
```

## What you see

- The required **criteria** (e.g. % of modules, % of approved assignments)
- Your **current progress** on each criterion
- A **Check eligibility** button to verify at any time
- The **issued certificate**, with a download link, once earned

## How to earn it

The certificate is issued **automatically** when you meet the rules defined by the class. When that happens, you receive a notification and can download the PDF.

Each certificate has a unique **number** and **verification code** that prove its authenticity.
