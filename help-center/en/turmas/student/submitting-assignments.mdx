title: "Submitting assignments (student)"
description: "See your class's assignments, submit your work in the requested format and resubmit when the mentor asks for adjustments."
---

Your class's **assignments** are located under **Assignments**, inside the class.

```
/member/class/{class}/assignments
```

The list shows each assignment, the deadline and the status of your submission.

## Submitting your work

Open the assignment to see the instructions and the **submission type** requested:

| Type | What to submit |
|---|---|
| **Text** | Written answer. |
| **File** | File upload (with a progress bar). |
| **Link** | URL. |
| **Video URL** | A video link. |
| **Any** | Any of the formats. |

Fill it in according to the type and submit.

## Feedback and resubmission

After the mentor reviews it, you're notified and see the **feedback** and the **grade** (if any). If the status is **needs revision** and resubmission is allowed, you can make adjustments and **resubmit**.
