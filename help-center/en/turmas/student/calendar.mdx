---
title: "Class calendar (student)"
description: "See your class's live sessions, deadlines and meetings, and sync everything with your external calendar via iCal."
---

The **calendar** shows all of your class's events — live sessions, deadlines, meetings and extra lessons.

```
/member/class/{class}/calendar
```

## What you see

- Upcoming events with date, time and type
- Access link (Zoom/stream) when available
- Events color-coded by type (live session, deadline, meeting, etc.)

Times appear in the **class's time zone**.

## Reminders

You receive automatic reminders before events (by email, in-app notification and push), as configured by the class team. Adjust your channels in **Settings → Notifications**.

## Sync with your calendar

Subscribe to the class's **iCal feed** to see events in Google Calendar, Outlook or Apple Calendar — they appear automatically and update when the class adds or changes events.
