title: "Content roadmap (student)"

---

The **roadmap** is the map of your class content. It shows the modules and lessons in order, your progress and what's still to come.

```
/member/class/{class}/roadmap
```

## Lesson states

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Completed** | You've already finished it. |
| **Available** | Released for you to access now. |
| **Locked** | Not released yet. |

When a lesson is locked by [scheduled release](/help-center/en/turmas/content-release-drip), the roadmap indicates **when** it will be released (e.g. "Released on [date]").

## Why is something locked?

In classes, content follows a schedule so everyone moves forward together. A lesson may open:

- after a number of days from the start of the class
- on a fixed date
- after you complete a previous lesson, module or assignment
