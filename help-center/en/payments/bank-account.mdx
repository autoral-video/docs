---
title: "Bank account"
description: "View and update the bank account that receives the transfers from your sales on your Pagar.me recipient."
---

The **bank account** is the destination for the transfers from your sales. You set it in the [recipient registration](/help-center/en/payments/setting-up-pagarme) and can update it later in the **Bank account** tab.

```
/admin/settings/pagarme
```

## Current account

When an account is already registered, the screen shows the details in a summarized and secure way:

- **Bank** (code)
- **Branch** with digit
- **Account** with the last 4 digits visible (e.g. ••••-1234)
- **Type**: checking or savings account

## Updating the account

Fill in the fields and save:

### Account holder

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Account holder name** | The account holder. |
| **Type** | Individual or company. |
| **Account holder's CPF/CNPJ** | The holder's document. |

### Bank details

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Bank** | Select from the list. |
| **Account type** | Checking or savings account. |
| **Branch** + **Digit** | Branch number (digit if applicable). |
| **Account number** + **Digit** | Account number and digit. |

Click **Save bank account**.

<Warning>
The bank account must belong to the same holder as the recipient. Mismatched details may be refused by Pagar.me.
</Warning>
