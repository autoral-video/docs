---
title: "Setting up transfers"
description: "Set how often your available balance is automatically transferred to your bank account: daily, weekly or monthly."
---

In addition to [manual withdrawals](/help-center/en/payments/balance-and-withdrawals), you can schedule **automatic transfers** of your available balance to your account. Configure this in the **Transfers** tab.

```
/admin/settings/pagarme
```

## Settings

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Automatic transfers** | Turns automatic balance transfer on or off. |
| **Interval** | Frequency: **Daily**, **Weekly** or **Monthly**. |
| **Day** | Depends on the interval (see below). |

### Day by interval

| Interval | Day |
|---|---|
| **Daily** | Not applicable. |
| **Weekly** | Day of the week, Monday to Friday. |
| **Monthly** | Day of the month (1 to 31). |

Click **Save settings**.

<Note>
With automatic transfers **turned off**, the available balance stays in the Pagar.me account until you [request a withdrawal manually](/help-center/en/payments/balance-and-withdrawals).
</Note>

<Warning>
Each transfer has a fixed fee per operation (see [Fees and MDR](/help-center/en/payments/fees-and-mdr)). More frequent intervals generate more fees.
</Warning>
