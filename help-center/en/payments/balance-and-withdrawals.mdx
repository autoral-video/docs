The **Balance** and **Receivables** tabs show how much you have to receive and let you request withdrawals.

```
/admin/settings/pagarme
```

## Balance

The **Balance** tab shows:

| Amount | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Available** | Released amount, ready for withdrawal. |
| **Pending** | Amount from sales still within the release period (e.g. credit card in D+30). |
| **Transferred** | Total already transferred to your account (history). |

<Note>
If a **stale balance** warning appears, it means the displayed amount came from the cache because Pagar.me was temporarily unavailable. Use **Refresh** to try fetching the most recent value.
</Note>

### Refresh balance

The **Refresh** button fetches the most recent balance, bypassing the cache. There is a minimum interval between refreshes.

### Balance unavailable

If you haven't completed the registration yet or the recipient is not **Active**, the balance appears as unavailable. See [Recipient status](/help-center/en/payments/recipient-status).

## Requesting a withdrawal

1. In the **Balance** tab, click **Request withdrawal**.
2. Enter the **amount** (minimum R$ 0.01, up to the available balance).
3. Click **Confirm withdrawal**.

The amount is transferred to the [registered bank account](/help-center/en/payments/bank-account). Each withdrawal has a transfer fee (see [Fees and MDR](/help-center/en/payments/fees-and-mdr)).

<Warning>
Withdrawals are only available when the recipient is **Active** and there is an **Available** balance greater than zero.
</Warning>

## Receivables

The **Receivables** tab shows a **schedule for the next 90 days** with the expected releases — a transparency requirement of Central Bank regulation.

For each date:

| Column | Content |
|---|---|
| **Date** | When the amount will be released. |
| **Type** | Origin (credit card, PIX, boleto...). |
| **Status** | Status of the release. |
| **Fee (R$)** | Fees deducted. |
| **Amount (R$)** | Amount to receive. |

In the footer: **Total to receive** and **Total fees**.
