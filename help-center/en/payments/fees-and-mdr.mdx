title: "Fees and MDR"
description: "Understand Pagar.me's fees (MDR per installment, fixed costs, PIX and boleto), the platform fee, and how interest-free or with-interest installments affect what you receive."
---

The **Fees** tab transparently shows all the fees that apply to your sales and the **settlement periods**.

```
/admin/settings/pagarme
```

<Note>
The amounts shown on this page are always the ones **currently in effect** for your account. The figures below are examples to explain the structure — check the screen for the current values.
</Note>

## Fixed costs per transaction

| Cost | When it applies | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Processing** | Per approved transaction | R$ 0.55 |
| **Antifraud** | Per credit card transaction | R$ 0.44 |
| **Transfer** | Per withdrawal/transfer | R$ 3.67 |

## Credit card MDR

The **MDR** is the percentage charged on the sale amount paid by card, and it grows with the number of installments. The screen shows the full table from **1x to 12x**. Examples:

| Installments | MDR (example) |
|---|---|
| 1x (single payment) | 3.89% |
| 6x | 12.36% |
| 12x | 21.24% |

## PIX and boleto

| Method | Fee (example) | Settlement |
|---|---|---|
| **PIX** | 1.09% of the amount | same day |
| **Boleto** | R$ 3.19 per transaction (+ refund fee) | on business days |

## Platform fee

In addition to Pagar.me's fees, the platform applies a **platform fee** (split) on the sale — shown on the screen when applicable, with the note "Applied in the split". It is autoral.me's transparent way of charging, with no hidden commission.

## Installments: interest-free vs with interest

The installment mode defines **who pays the interest** on the installments:

| Mode | The customer pays | You absorb | Highlight on screen |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Interest-free** | total amount ÷ installments | MDR + fixed costs + platform fee | **You receive** (net amount) |
| **With interest** | amount plus the interest | only the platform fee | **Customer pays** (final amount) |

The installment table shows, for each option: the MDR (plus the platform fee), the total cost and — depending on the mode — **how much you receive** or **how much the customer pays**.

## Settlement periods

| Method | Period (example) |
|---|---|
| **Credit card** | settles in D+30 (automatic anticipation may apply) |
| **Boleto** | a few business days |
| **PIX** | same day |

The expected receivables appear in the [receivables schedule](/help-center/en/payments/balance-and-withdrawals).
