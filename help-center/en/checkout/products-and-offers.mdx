---
title: "Products and Offers (deprecated)"
description: "The separate Product → Offer → Checkout flow was replaced by the Link Builder."
---

<Warning>
This flow is deprecated. You no longer need to create a product and an offer separately.
</Warning>

You now create everything in a single form with the **Link Builder**: when you choose the course, the product and offer are generated automatically.

➡️ [Creating a sales link](/help-center/en/checkout/creating-a-link)
➡️ [Advanced link settings](/help-center/en/checkout/advanced-settings)
