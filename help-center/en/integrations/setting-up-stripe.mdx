Stripe is the platform's primary payment gateway. It allows you to process sales in multiple currencies and international payment methods, making it ideal for reaching students anywhere in the world with an optimized checkout experience.

The platform uses **Stripe Connect** to link your Stripe account via OAuth. This means you connect with a single click — no API keys to copy, no webhooks to configure manually. Everything is set up automatically.

## What you'll need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Stripe account — sign up at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com)
- Access to the **Stripe Dashboard** (dashboard.stripe.com)

## Step 1: Go to Stripe settings

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Stripe**

You will see a connection panel with a **Connect with Stripe** button.

## Step 2: Connect your Stripe account

1. Click **Connect with Stripe**
2. You will be redirected to the Stripe authorization page
3. Log in to your Stripe account (or create one)
4. Review the permissions requested and click **Authorize access**
5. You will be redirected back to the platform

Once redirected, the panel will display a **Stripe connected** confirmation badge along with your account identifier.

<Note>Your API keys and webhook configuration are set up automatically — no manual credentials are required.</Note>

## Step 3: Enable Stripe

After connecting, toggle **Enable Stripe** to activate it as a payment method in your checkouts.

<Note>Stripe will only be available at checkout after it is both connected and enabled.</Note>

## Managing your connection

### Disabling Stripe

You can disable Stripe at any time by toggling **Enable Stripe** off. Your account remains connected — you can re-enable it without going through the OAuth flow again.

### Disconnecting your account

To fully remove the Stripe integration:

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Stripe**
2. Click **Disconnect**
3. Confirm the action

This revokes the OAuth authorization and removes all stored credentials. To use Stripe again, you will need to go through the Connect flow once more.

## Quick reference

| Action | Where |
|---|---|
| Connect Stripe account | Admin → Settings → Stripe → **Connect with Stripe** |
| Enable / disable at checkout | Admin → Settings → Stripe → **Enable Stripe** toggle |
| Disconnect account | Admin → Settings → Stripe → **Disconnect** |
