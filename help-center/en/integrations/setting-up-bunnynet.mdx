Bunny.net is the platform's video streaming provider. It delivers your course videos through a global CDN, ensuring fast and reliable playback for your students anywhere in the world.

This guide walks you through finding each credential in the Bunny.net dashboard and entering it into your platform settings.

## Billing model

Bunny.net works on a **pay-as-you-go** model: you maintain a credit balance in your account and services are charged as you use them. There is no fixed monthly fee.

### Free trial

Every new account receives **$20 in credits** with no payment card required. Adding a payment method unlocks an additional **$30**, for a total of **$50 in credits** to explore the platform.

The trial period lasts **14 days**. During this time, credits cover all services: video storage, transcoding, and CDN delivery.

<Note>After 14 days, trial credits expire. To continue, simply recharge your balance using the **Recharge Your Account** button in the Bunny.net dashboard.</Note>

### Balance alerts

Bunny.net sends automatic email notifications when your balance is running low:

| Account type | Alerts sent |
|---|---|
| No payment card | When $10 and $1 remain |
| Payment card added | When $20 and $5 remain |

<Note>Add a payment method to receive earlier alerts and avoid interruptions to your course streaming.</Note>

## What you'll need

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Bunny.net account — sign up at [bunny.net](https://bunny.net)
- A **Stream Library** created in your Bunny.net account

## Step 1: Get your Account API Key

The API Key is the master credential that connects your platform to your Bunny.net account. It is used for all API operations including video uploads.

1. Log in to [dash.bunny.net](https://bunny.net?ref=dr5fu0elyi)
2. Click your account name or avatar in the **top-left corner**
3. Navigate to **Account settings → API Key**
4. Click the **eye icon** to reveal the key, or the **copy icon** to copy it directly to your clipboard
5. Paste it into the **API Key** field in your platform settings

<Warning>**Security note:** This key grants full access to your Bunny.net account. Never share it publicly or commit it to version control.</Warning>

## Step 2: Get your Stream Library ID

The Library ID identifies the video library where your course videos are stored and transcoded. Each library has a unique numeric ID.

### If you already have a library:

1. In the Bunny.net dashboard, go to **Stream** in the left sidebar
2. Click on your video library
3. Open the **API** tab inside the library
4. The **Library ID** is displayed at the top of the page — copy it
5. Paste it into the **Library ID** field in your platform settings
6. Then copy the **Pull Zone** URL shown in the same block
7. Paste it into the **Pull Zone** field in your platform settings

### If you need to create a library first:

1. Go to **Stream → Add Video Library**
2. Enter a name for your library
3. Select at least **2 storage regions** for data durability and performance
4. Click **Add Video Library**
5. Then follow the steps above to retrieve the ID and Pull Zone from the **API** tab

## Quick reference

| Platform Field | Where to find it in Bunny.net |
|---|---|
| **API Key** | Account → API Key |
| **Library ID** | Stream → [Your Library] → API tab |
| **Pull Zone** | Stream → [Your Library] → API tab → Pull Zone |

## Saving your settings

After filling in all fields, click **Save**. A brief confirmation message will appear. Your platform will now use Bunny.net for all video uploads and streaming.

If videos fail to upload after saving, double-check that the **Library ID** and **API Key** are correct — an incorrect Library ID is the most common cause of upload failures.
