## Courses and Lessons

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a course from scratch?">
    Go to **Admin → Courses → New Course**, fill in the basic information (name, slug, description), create modules and, inside each module, create lessons. Each lesson can be of type **video**, **article** or **quiz**. See the full guide at [Creating a Course](/help-center/en/courses/creating-a-course).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I offer free lessons as a sample?">
    Yes. On the lesson's **Settings** tab, enable the **Free** option. The lesson becomes available as a preview even for visitors who haven't been granted access to the course.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does sequential progression work?">
    Enable **Sequential Lessons** and/or **Sequential Modules** in the course settings. Students can only advance to the next lesson/module after completing the current one. The first lesson of each module is always accessible, even with sequential progression enabled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the size limit for videos and materials?">
    Videos are uploaded to Bunny.net (no practical limit). Supplementary materials are limited to **300 MB** per file. Course thumbnails: up to 2 MB. Background images: up to 4 MB.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use YouTube or Vimeo videos instead of Bunny.net?">
    Yes. On the lesson's **Video** tab, paste the video URL or the `<iframe>` code into the embed field. The system automatically converts YouTube and Vimeo URLs to the correct format.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Checkout and Sales

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a purchase page?">
    Use the **Link Builder** under **Admin → Link Builder → New link**. In a single form you choose the course, set the price and payment methods — the product and offer are created automatically. See [Creating a sales link](/help-center/en/checkout/creating-a-link).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to create a product and offer separately?">
    No. The old Product → Offer → Checkout flow is deprecated. When you pick the course in the Link Builder, the product and offer are generated behind the scenes. You only think about the link you want to sell.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which currencies can I sell in?">
    In **Brazilian Real (BRL)**. Payments are processed by Pagar.me. The available methods are card (single or installments), PIX and boleto.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between up-sell and cross-sell?">
    **Up-sell** replaces the main product with a superior offer before completing the purchase (does not add). **Cross-sell** adds complementary products to the current purchase (sums to the total). Access is granted individually for each accepted cross-sell. Configure under [Advanced link settings](/help-center/en/checkout/advanced-settings).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I share the sales link?">
    After saving, the public URL is `https://yourdomain.com/checkout/{slug}`. Share it directly or link it on the course page under **Admin → Courses → [Course] → Settings**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I configure refunds?">
    On each link, under **Advanced settings**, enable **Allow refund** and set the **Refund deadline (days)**. Requests are reviewed under **Admin → Finance → Refund requests**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I receive payments from my sales?">
    Payments are processed by **Pagar.me**. You create a **recipient** (marketplace model) by providing your details — individual or company — and the destination bank account, under **Admin → Settings → Payout Details**. See [Setting up payments](/help-center/en/payments/setting-up-pagarme).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which payment methods can I accept?">
    **Credit card** (single or up to 12x installments), **PIX** and **boleto** — all via Pagar.me. PIX confirms in seconds; card and boleto follow their settlement timelines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When are funds available and how do I withdraw?">
    Each sale stays as **pending** until the settlement period (credit usually D+30; PIX same day), then becomes **available balance**. With the recipient **Active**, request a withdrawal to your account on the **Balance** tab, or schedule [automatic transfers](/help-center/en/payments/transfers). See [Balance and withdrawals](/help-center/en/payments/balance-and-withdrawals).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does the platform charge?">
    A **platform fee** (a 0.5% split) applied transparently to the sale, on top of Pagar.me's own fees (card MDR, PIX, boleto and fixed costs). No hidden commission. See the full table under [Fees and MDR](/help-center/en/payments/fees-and-mdr).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My recipient is 'under review' or was refused. What do I do?">
    Pagar.me runs a credentialing review (**KYC**). You can sell during the initial statuses, but you can only withdraw once **Active**. Track pending items, messages and the **liveness verification** link under [Recipient status](/help-center/en/payments/recipient-status).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get notified of new sales?">
    After each confirmed sale, the admin automatically receives an alert email. Outgoing webhooks can also be configured under **Admin → Settings → Webhooks** for integration with external systems.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Students and Access

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I import students from another platform?">
    Go to **Admin → Settings → Import / Export**, upload the CSV exported from the original platform (Hotmart, Kiwify, Eduzz, Monetizze) and use the matching **preset** for automatic mapping. See [Importing and Exporting Students](/help-center/en/students/importing-and-exporting-students).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I manually grant access to a student?">
    Yes. Under **Admin → Students**, search for the student and use **Manage Accesses** to grant, revoke or extend access to specific courses with lifetime or temporary type.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does temporary access work?">
    Set **Type: Temporary** and select the duration (30, 90, 180, 365 days, or custom). Access expires automatically on the calculated date. For subscriptions, access is managed by the billing cycle — active while the subscription is active.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are Classes (turmas)?">
    **Classes** turn a course into an experience with a **start date, limited seats, its own community and active accompaniment** (cohort model). By linking a class to a sales link, all buyers are automatically enrolled in it. See [What are classes](/help-center/en/turmas/what-are-classes).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can I do inside a class?">
    Release content on a schedule ([drip](/help-center/en/turmas/content-release-drip)), create [events and live sessions with reminders](/help-center/en/turmas/calendar-and-events), assign [assignments and review submissions](/help-center/en/turmas/assignments), issue [automatic certificates](/help-center/en/turmas/certificates), [track engagement](/help-center/en/turmas/tracking-engagement) and assign [mentors and tutors](/help-center/en/turmas/mentors-and-tutors).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the waitlist work when a class is full?">
    Limited-seat classes can capture interested people in a **waitlist**. When a spot opens, you promote someone from the queue, who receives a confirmation link with a deadline. See [Waitlist](/help-center/en/turmas/waitlist).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Marketing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a discount coupon?">
    Go to **Admin → Marketing → Coupons → New Coupon**. Set the code, discount percentage, validity, usage limits and eligible products. See the guide at [Creating Coupons](/help-center/en/marketing/creating-coupons).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I offer different discounts per number of installments?">
    Yes, when card installments are enabled. On the coupon, configure **Installment Discount** with ranges (e.g., 1 installment = 25%, 2-6 = 15%, 7-12 = 10%) to incentivize upfront payments.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is cart abandonment?">
    The platform captures lead emails at checkout and triggers automatic coupons for those who didn't complete the purchase. Configure under **Admin → Marketing → Cart Abandonment**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I add tracking pixels (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel)?">
    Use the **Script Manager** under **Admin → Settings → Scripts**. Paste the pixel code into the desired position (global head, purchase confirmation footer, etc.). Dynamic variables like `{{checkout_id}}` and `{{product_price}}` are available for conversion events.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I customize the platform theme?">
    Go to **Admin → Settings → Theme** to change colors, logo, favicon and visual elements. Changes reflect immediately across the entire public and members area.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have my own domain?">
    Yes. The platform is multi-tenant and each account operates on a dedicated domain. Configure under **Admin → Settings → Domain**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I find the database backup?">
    Automatic backups are displayed under **Admin → Settings → Backup Monitor**. Backups are generated at regular intervals and are available for download.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    Open a ticket under **Admin → Help Center**. The team replies by email and within the platform itself.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
