---

The **community** is where you talk with other students in groups.

```
/member/community
```

Access it through the **Community** link in the menu. If the link doesn't appear, the community may be disabled by your platform.

## Finding groups

In the sidebar, you see the list of groups you have access to, with an icon, name, and a count of posts and members. Use the **search** to filter by name. A **padlock** indicates a group you don't participate in yet.

## How you join a group

It depends on the type of group:

| Type | How you join |
|---|---|
| **Open to the whole community** | Automatic — every student with active access participates. |
| **Linked to classes/courses** | Automatic when you enroll in the linked class or course. |
| **Invite only** | The administrator adds you manually. |

## Public vs private

- **Public** groups appear in the list for all members.
- **Private** groups appear only for those who have access.

<Note>
In groups linked to classes/courses, access appears shortly after enrollment. If you just bought and don't see the group yet, wait a few moments.
</Note>
