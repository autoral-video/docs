Inside a group, you participate by posting, commenting, and reacting. See [Accessing the community](/help-center/en/community/student/accessing-community) to find the groups.

## Creating a post

In the post field, write your message and send it. A post can have:

| Content | Details |
|---|---|
| **Text** | Up to 5000 characters. |
| **Media** | Images (JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF — up to 5 MB each) and videos (MP4 — up to 300 MB each). |
| **Poll** | A question with 2 or more options. |

### Polls

When creating a poll, set the **question** and the **options**. You can allow **multiple choice** and set a **closing time**. Results appear in real time; once closed, the poll no longer accepts votes.

## Commenting and replying

Open a post's comments to comment. You can **reply** to a comment, creating a threaded conversation. Comments load gradually — use "load more" and "see more replies".

## Reactions

React to posts and comments with one of the emojis:

| Emoji | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 👍 | Like |
| ❤️ | Love |
| 🎉 | Celebrate |
| 😂 | Funny |
| 🤔 | Thoughtful |
| 🙌 | Clap |

Click again to remove your reaction.

## Mentioning people

Type **@** and the name to mention someone in the group. The mentioned person receives a **notification** (by email and in the app) with the link to the post.

## Pinned posts

Posts with the **Pinned** badge appear at the top of the feed — they are highlighted by moderators because they're important. Each group can have up to 3.

## Notifications and digests

Besides mentions, you can receive a **digest** of the group's news — **daily** or **weekly**, or disable it. Adjust it in **Settings → Notifications**.
