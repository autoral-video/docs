---
title: "Moderating the community"
description: "Pin important posts, hide or delete posts and comments, and keep order in the community groups."
---

Moderators and administrators keep order in the groups directly in the feed, in **Admin**, or in the members area itself.

Who can moderate: **administrators**, group **moderators**, and **mentors** of a linked class. See [Members and moderators](/help-center/en/community/members-and-moderators).

## Pinning posts

Use **Pin** on a post to highlight it at the **top of the feed**. To remove it, use **Unpin**.

<Note>
Each group can have at most **3 pinned posts** at the same time.
</Note>

## Hide vs delete

You can moderate both **posts** and **comments**:

| Action | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Hide** | Disappears from the feed but stays on record. Useful for removing something without deleting it permanently. |
| **Delete** | Goes to the trash. When you delete a root comment, the replies are deleted too. |

<Warning>
Deleted posts and comments stay in the **trash for 30 days** before permanent removal (along with images and attachments).
</Warning>

## Who can moderate what

- **Members** moderate only their own content.
- **Moderators** and **administrators** pin, hide, and delete anyone's content.

See the full permissions table in [Members and moderators](/help-center/en/community/members-and-moderators).
