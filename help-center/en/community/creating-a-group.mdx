---
title: "Creating and configuring a group"
description: "Create a community group by defining its name, icon, description, visibility, and access mode (open, linked to classes/courses, or invite only)."
---

**Groups** organize the community by topic, class, or course. Create one in **Admin → Community → Groups → New group**.

```
/admin/community/groups
```

## Group fields

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Group name** | Name shown to members. E.g.: "Welcome", "Class 2026", "Mastermind". Required (up to 120 characters). |
| **Icon (emoji)** | Emoji that identifies the group. E.g.: 🚀. Optional. |
| **Description** | What the group is about. Optional (up to 1000 characters). |
| **Visibility** | **Public** or **Private** (see below). |
| **Who participates** | The group's access mode (see below). |
| **Group active** | Deactivating hides the group from all members. Enabled by default. |

## Visibility

| Option | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Public** | The group appears in the list for all community members. |
| **Private** | The group only appears for those who have access. |

## Access modes (Who participates)

Defines **who joins** the group:

| Mode | Who joins |
|---|---|
| **Open to the whole community** | Any student with active access joins automatically. |
| **Linked to classes/courses** | Only active students of the linked classes or courses join automatically. |
| **Invite only** | Only members added manually by the administrator. |

### Linking classes and courses

When you choose **Linked to classes/courses**, these fields appear:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Linked classes** | Active students of the selected classes get access automatically. |
| **Linked courses** | Active students of the selected courses get access automatically. |

<Note>
This is the mode used by the [class community](/help-center/en/turmas/class-community): link the group to the class and enrollment alone grants access.
</Note>

### Invite only

When you choose **Invite only**, you define the **members** manually — either in the form itself or later in [Members and moderators](/help-center/en/community/members-and-moderators).

## Saving

Click **Save**. The group then appears in the list and, depending on its visibility and access mode, for students.
