title: "Enabling and managing the community"
description: "Turn on your platform's community, view the list of groups, and manage the status, editing, and removal of each group."
---

The **community** is the space where your students talk in **groups** — by class, by course, or open to everyone. This page explains how to turn on the community and manage the groups.

## Enabling the community

Go to **Admin → Community → Settings**.

```
/admin/community/settings
```

| Setting | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Community enabled** | When turned on, students with any active access see and post in the groups they have access to. Turning it off **hides the "Community" link** from the students' menu. |

## The list of groups

Go to **Admin → Community → Groups**.

```
/admin/community/groups
```

- **Search** by group name
- **Filter** by status: active / inactive
- Each group appears as a card with an icon, name, description, **visibility** badge, **access mode** badge, and a count of **posts** and **members**.

## Actions for each group

| Action | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Edit** | Opens the group form. See [Creating a group](/help-center/en/community/creating-a-group). |
| **Members** | Manages members (only for groups in **Invite only** mode). See [Members and moderators](/help-center/en/community/members-and-moderators). |
| **Activate / Deactivate** | Deactivating hides the group from all members, without deleting it. |
| **Remove** | Moves the group and its posts to the trash. |

<Warning>
When you remove a group, it and its posts stay in the **trash for 30 days** and are then permanently deleted, along with images and attachments. Until then, recovery is possible.
</Warning>

## Create a new group

Click **New group** in the list. See the step-by-step in [Creating a group](/help-center/en/community/creating-a-group).
